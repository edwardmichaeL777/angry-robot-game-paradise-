First:The Martingale Strategy Explained

The Martingale strategy is a betting system based on the principle of doubling your bet after every loss.

Detailed Betting Progression:(a little example: the win rate is 50 percent,and your bet will doubled if you win.)
  • First Round:
    • Initial bet: $1,000
    • Win: Receive $1,000 back + $1,000 bonus
    • Lose: Lose entire $1,000
  • Second Round (if first round lost):
    • New bet: $2,000
    • Win: Receive $2,000 back + $2,000 bonus(1,000 recover previous lost, 1,000 for the money you win.)
    • Lose: Total losses now $3,000 ($1,000 + $2,000)
  • Third Round (if second round lost):
    • New bet: $4,000
    • Win: Receive $4,000 back + $4,000 (3,000 recover previous lost, 1,000 for the money you win.)
    • Lose: Total losses now $7,000 ($1,000 + $2,000 + $4,000)
  • Fourth Round (if third round lost):
    • New bet: $8,000
    • and so on

The core idea is that by doubling your bet, you'll eventually recover all previous losses and make a profit when you win.
